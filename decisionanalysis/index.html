Chapter I

Executive Summary

Engineered a quantitative go-to-market framework using AHP across 4 matrices. Pinpointed the B2B sector (46.6%) as the primary growth engine, driven by a 40% weight on revenue potential. Optimized acquisition via Co-branding Partnerships (27.1%), leveraging a 48.2% focus on brand trust. Secured supply chain resilience with Supplier B (49.9%), prioritizing quality (51.8%) over raw capacity. Crowned the E-S35 (40.0%) as the flagship vehicle, balancing a 38.2% price-sensitivity requirement. This rigorous analysis eliminated subjective bias, deploying an optimized strategy across 18+ decision variables.
